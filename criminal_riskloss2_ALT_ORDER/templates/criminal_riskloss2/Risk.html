For the first module, you will select from among seven different gambles the one gamble you would like to play.

  • You must select one and only one of these gambles.
  • To select a gamble, select the radio button next to the gamble you prefer and then click the Next button.

After you submit your choice, the computer will use a random number generator to generate a number between 1 and 100. Each number has an equal probability of being generated. If the number is less than 50, then the lower of the two amounts will be chosen for payment. If the number is greater than or equal to 50, then the higher of the two amounts will be chosen for payment.
